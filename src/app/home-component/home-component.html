2. Work Philosophy (PSW)

My work philosophy as a Personal Support Worker is based on respect, dignity, compassion, and client-centered care. I believe every individual deserves to be treated with kindness, regardless of their health condition or background.

As a PSW, I value empathy, patience, and strong communication skills. I believe in supporting clients to maintain their independence while ensuring their safety and well-being. I also believe teamwork is very important, and I aim to collaborate with nurses and other healthcare professionals to provide the best quality care.

Core Values:

  • Respect: Treating every client with dignity and honor
  • Compassion: Understanding and caring for clients' needs
  • Communication: Clear, empathetic, and professional interaction
  • Teamwork: Collaborating effectively with healthcare professionals
  • Independence: Supporting clients to maintain their autonomy

3. Career Goals (2–5 Years)

My goal in the next 2–5 years is to build a strong career in the healthcare field as a Personal Support Worker.

In the next 2 years, I plan to:

In the next 5 years, I aim to:
